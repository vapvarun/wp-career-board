**Trigger:** User navigates to employer applications view multiple times in one week (signal: actively managing hiring)

**Subject A:** Your hiring is outgrowing 5 statuses

**Body:**

Hi [First Name],

If you're actively moving candidates through your hiring funnel, the 5-status system in the free version works — but it doesn't show you the full picture.

WP Career Board Pro's **Application Pipeline** gives you:

- A Kanban board where each column is a hiring stage
- Custom stages — name them whatever your process is (Phone Screen, Technical Test, Panel Interview, Offer)
- Drag candidates between columns to move them forward
- Terminal stages (Hired / Rejected) that trigger automatic emails and update your counters
- Stage history — a log of every status change with timestamps

The stages are per-board, so if you run multiple job boards they each have their own pipeline.

**Trigger:** User has 10+ jobs across significantly different categories (signal: might benefit from board separation)

**Subject A:** Running multiple job categories? There's a cleaner way.
**Subject B:** Separate job boards, one WordPress install

**Body:**

Hi [First Name],

If you're running jobs across multiple categories — tech roles, marketing roles, remote-only listings — you can keep them all in one board. But a lot of boards find it cleaner to separate them.

WP Career Board Pro's **Multi-Board Engine** lets you:

- Create as many boards as you need ("Tech Jobs", "Marketing", "Remote Only")
- Each board has its own jobs, employers, pipeline stages, and (optionally) credit pricing
- A **Board Switcher block** lets visitors tab between boards on one page — no page reload
- Admins can assign employers to specific boards

All from one WordPress install, one admin.

**Trigger:** Site has 50+ published jobs (signal: board may be ready to monetize)

**Subject A:** You're running a real job board. Are you charging for it?
**Subject B:** Add a revenue stream to your job board

**Body:**

Hi [First Name],

A job board with consistent job postings is a product employers will pay for — especially when it's in a niche community they care about.

WP Career Board Pro includes a **Credit System** powered by Stripe:

- Create credit packages at any price (e.g., 1 job post for $29, 5 posts for $99)
- Employers buy credits and spend them to post jobs
- Per-board pricing — you can charge different rates on different boards
- Stripe Checkout handles the payment; a webhook confirms it and adds credits automatically

No transaction fee from us. Stripe's standard rate applies.

**Subject A:** Let employers find candidates — not just wait for applications
**Subject B:** Resume search: flip the hiring model

**Body:**

Hi [First Name],

Most job boards are one-directional — employers post, candidates apply. WP Career Board Pro flips it.

With the **Resume Builder + Resume Search:**

- Candidates build a structured resume inside WordPress — experience, education, skills, portfolio links
- Employers search the resume database and filter by skills, location, and experience level
- Candidate profiles are visible only to employers (configurable)

It gives your community's employers a reason to keep coming back even when they're not actively posting a job.
